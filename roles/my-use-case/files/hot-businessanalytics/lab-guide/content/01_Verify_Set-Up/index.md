## Verify setup

Before we begin, let's verify that you can access the application. 

1. *Access* **Dynatrace University** 
    - *Open* [**university.dynatrace.com**](university.dynatrace.com)
    - *Log in* with your **account credentials** 
    - *Open* the **Business Analytics event** in the section **Upcoming events** 
2. *Verify* **environment** 
    - *Verify* **access** to the Dynatrace Environment 
3. About the demo application 
    - **Partner PowerUp BizObs** simulates customer journey analytics across various industries, so here are some things you can do in the app: 
        - **Start** customer journeys with different templates
        - **Monitor** real-time business metrics 
        - **Simulate** various business scenarios
        - View **comprehensive analytics** dashboards
    - Make sure you can access your Partner PowerUp BizObs app by:  
      1. *Clicking* on **Partner PowerUp BizObs** link inside your terminal 
      2. *Navigate* to the **Dashboard** section
      3. *Click* "**Start Journey**"  
      4. *Select* an **industry template** (e.g., Retail, Banking, eCommerce)
      5. *Configure* journey **parameters** (users, interval, error rate)
      6. *Click* "**Begin Simulation**" 
      7. *Monitor* the **real-time metrics** and **step completions**
